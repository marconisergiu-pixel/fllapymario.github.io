📚 HELP & SUPPORT

❓ How do I play?

Click or press SPACE to make the bird jump. Avoid the orange and blue pipes. Each pair of pipes you pass increases your score by 1. Try to get the highest score!

❓ What's my goal?

Stay alive as long as possible and pass through as many pipes as you can. Your score increases with each successful pipe. No level limit!

❓ Why did I die?

You hit a pipe or touched the top/bottom of the screen. Be careful with your jumps and timing!

❓ Is my score saved?

Currently, scores are saved during your session. Reload the page and you start fresh. (Future: Online leaderboard!)

❓ Can I change difficulty?

Right now there's one difficulty level. Future versions may include Easy/Hard modes!

⚙️ GAME RULES

  1. Click or Press SPACE to make the bird jump
  2. Gravity pulls down continuously - keep jumping!
  3. Avoid pipes - both orange and blue
  4. Avoid boundaries - top and bottom of screen
  5. Score increases each time you pass a pipe pair
  6. Game ends when you hit an obstacle

📊 SCORING

  • ✓ Each pipe pair passed = +1 Point
  • ✓ No time limit - play as long as you can!
  • ✓ Try to beat your high score

⚠️ The bird won't jump

Make sure you're clicking on the game area or pressing SPACE. Both should work!

⚠️ Game is running too fast/slow

This depends on your browser and computer. Try closing other tabs or reloading the page.

⚠️ I can't see the bird

The bird is shown as 🐦. If you don't see it, your browser might not support emojis. Try using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.

⚠️ Collision detection feels unfair

The game checks collision very strictly. Small touches count as hits. This makes the game challenging but fair!

⚠️ Something else is broken

Try:
1. Reload the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Report issue on GitHub
